WHAT IS JOYFUL LIVING FROM THE HEART?

 

Joyful living from the heart is intentional living rather than living by default. It involves, mindfulness, following your intuition and using your heart consciously, actively and dynamically to have dominion over your health, your happiness and to live your greatest potential. Living from the heart gives you the resilience to be happy regardless of the circumstances around you.

Each person has three brains, the head, heart and gut brain. The gut brain is your resource for body awareness and intuition. Living from the heart integrates all three brains for expanded awareness and intentional living. This is the how and tao of joyful living. Living from the heart is both a science and an art.

About Elisheva Herrera

Elisheva Herrera is Chief Joyologist of Happy People Happy Planet™. She offers transformational wisdom, and joy via retreats and workshops, sound healing, heart coaching, lectures and inspirational performances.

Elisheva is a Certified HeartMath Resilience Advantage™ Trainer, Reiki practitioner, Sound Healer, Laughter Yoga Leader and Vocalist. She is a “Grounded Intuitive” with a B.A. in Economics and minor in Music from Tufts University. Elisheva has been a tutor/teacher of many subjects since the age of 7. While grateful for book smarts and years of teaching experiences, Elisheva sees joyful living as her greatest accomplishment. She lives a magical life by following her heart and teaching others to do the same.
